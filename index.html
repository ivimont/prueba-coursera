Paella consists of round grain rice, bajoqueta and tavella (varieties of green beans), rabbit, chicken, sometimes duck and garrofó (a variety of lima or butter bean) cooked in olive oil and chicken broth. Also have saffron, the colorant. Includes drink and dessert of your choice. Paella

Spanish omelette is a traditional dish from Spain and one of the signature dishes in the Spanish cuisine. It is an omelette made with eggs and potatoes, optionally including onion. Includes drink and dessert of your choice. Tortilla

Cocido madrileño is a traditional chickpea-based stew from Madrid, Spain. A substantial dish prepared with meat and vegetables, it is most popular during the winter but is served throughout the year too in some restaurants. Includes drink and dessert of your choice. Cocido
